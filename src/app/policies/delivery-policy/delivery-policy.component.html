Delivery Policy

Last Updated: August 5, 2024


Welcome to Roleplay Delight's Delivery Policy, where we detail the procedures and standards governing the delivery of our lingerie. This policy outlines our approach to delivery logistics, including the use of third-party services, cancellation procedures, and customer communication protocols. By engaging with our website and services, you agree to adhere to the terms and guidelines set forth in this Delivery Policy. We are committed to ensuring a seamless and reliable delivery experience for all our valued customers.

  • We do not offer cash on delivery. Our delivery process is facilitated through a third-party service provider.

  • If you choose to cancel your order while it is in transit for delivery, a cancellation fee will be applicable.

  • In the event that you are not available either at the designated pickup point or your specified delivery address, the package will be returned to us.

  • Prior to initiating delivery, we will contact you via phone 15 minutes beforehand. During this timeframe, you have the option to cancel your order without incurring any cancellation fees.
